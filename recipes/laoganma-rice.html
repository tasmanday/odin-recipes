Lao Gan Ma Rice

Description

This recipe is for a super fast and easy rice dish. it involves only 4 ingredients and the longest step is cooking the rice which can be done ahead of time if needed.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white rice
  • 1 egg
  • 1.5 tsp Lao gan ma chilli oil
  • spring onion (to garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook the rice according to the instructions on the packet.
  2. When the rice is almost done fry the egg.
  3. Once the rice and egg are cooked plate up with rice first, then chilli oil, then egg, and lastly sprinkle chopped spring onions on top
